# Zachary's Karate Club

Nodes: Members of the Karate Club<br/>
Edges: Interactions between members outside the club

### Important Features
* Undirected
* Unweighted
* 34 nodes
* 78 edges
* Nodes are indexed from 1

**Note**: Node 1 represents the Instructor and Node 34 the Administrator.
